PRODUCT VISION (PRD)

The Problem: Remote work has stripped the "office" of its spatial context. Teams are isolated in vertical silos (Slack channels, Zoom links), losing the horizontal visibility that drives culture and serendipity.

The Solution: Virtual Office is a spatial operating system. It visualizes the team on a floor plan, allowing for fluid movement between deep work, casual chat, and intense collaboration.

CORE PILLARS

  • Spatial Awareness: See who is where and what they are doing.
  • Fluid Communication: Zero-friction transition from text to voice.
  • AI-Augmented: Meeting notes, summaries, and context are automatic.
